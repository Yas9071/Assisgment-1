My Phone Usage Journey

My phone has quietly become one of the most consistent tools in my daily life. From early morning alarms to late-night scrolling, it holds my routines, conversations, creativity, and distractions. Over the years, my screen time hasn’t just measured usage — it has reflected phases of growth, stress, productivity, and rest. This project explores the patterns behind my phone habits and what they reveal about me.

Total Screen Time (2020–2025)

Since 2020, my daily screen time has fluctuated depending on where I was in life. Some years were shaped by remote learning and long hours online, while others reflected a more balanced routine between work, school, and personal life. This visualization captures my average daily screen time across the past years — highlighting how my digital behavior evolved alongside my responsibilities and environment.

App Category Breakdown in 2024

Not all screen time is equal. Some hours are spent on productivity and learning, while others go to social media, entertainment, or communication. In 2024, my most-used app categories reveal a lot about my priorities. This chart explores where my time actually goes and how intentional (or unintentional) those habits are.
